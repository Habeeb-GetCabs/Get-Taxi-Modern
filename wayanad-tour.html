Wayanad Premium Tour

Step into a sub-tropical wilderness. Wayanad offers a unique blend of ancient history, massive dams, and deep Kerala forests. This 4-day itinerary ensures you cover the vast distances between its incredible sights with complete comfort.

Tour Itinerary

01

The Ghat Drive & Arrival

Start your journey from Coimbatore, driving through the beautiful Nilgiri biosphere towards Kerala. Experience the famous Thamarassery Churam (hairpin bends) and take in the panoramic views before settling into your resort.

  • Scenic drive via Gudalur or Palakkad
  • Lakkidi Viewpoint (Gateway of Wayanad)
  • Pookode Lake (Boating)
  • Check-in and evening relaxation
02

Dams, Ziplines & Waterfalls

Get ready for an adventurous day. Visit the largest earth dam in India and optionally partake in thrilling activities like ziplining before cooling off at a majestic waterfall.

  • Banasura Sagar Dam (Speed boating)
  • Karlad Lake (Zipline & Kayaking)
  • Meenmutty Waterfalls or Soochipara Falls
  • Return to resort
03

History & Wildlife Safari

Step back in time to explore ancient petroglyphs carved into a massive cave system. In the afternoon, head into the dense forest for a chance to spot wild elephants, bison, and deer.

  • Edakkal Caves (Requires a moderate uphill trek)
  • Wayanad Heritage Museum
  • Muthanga Wildlife Sanctuary (Afternoon Jeep Safari)
04

Islands & Departure

Check out of your accommodation after breakfast. Spend your final morning exploring a beautiful river island known for its rare orchids before driving safely back to Coimbatore.

  • Kuruvadweep (Kuruva Island)
  • Optional spice shopping
  • Comfortable drop-off at Coimbatore (Airport/Railway/Home)
