Coorg Premium Tour

Discover the "Scotland of India." Famous for its endless coffee plantations, misty valleys, and rich Kodava culture, this extended 4-day tour allows you to comfortably explore the absolute best of Karnataka's premier hill station.

Tour Itinerary

01

Journey to Coffee County

Depart early from Coimbatore for a scenic drive towards Karnataka. Before reaching Madikeri (Coorg), we will stop at the magnificent Golden Temple to experience a slice of Tibetan culture.

  • Drive via Dhimbam Ghats / Mysore route
  • Namdroling Monastery (Golden Temple, Bylakuppe)
  • Check-in to your resort/homestay in Madikeri
  • Evening at leisure to enjoy the cool climate
02

Waterfalls & Panoramas

A day dedicated to nature. Visit the famous Abbey Falls and head out for an adventurous viewpoint climb. End the day watching a beautiful sunset from the historic Raja's Seat.

  • Abbey Falls
  • Mandalpatti Viewpoint (Optional Jeep Safari)
  • Madikeri Fort
  • Raja's Seat (Sunset view and musical fountain)
03

Elephants & Bamboo Forests

Head towards Kushalnagar to interact with majestic elephants on the banks of the Kaveri river, followed by a peaceful walk through a dense island forest.

  • Dubare Elephant Camp (River crossing & feeding)
  • Nisargadhama (Bamboo forest island & hanging bridge)
  • Omkareshwara Temple
  • Evening local market visit for coffee & spice shopping
04

Heritage & Departure

After breakfast, check out of your resort. If time permits, we can make a brief stop at Mysore before completing the return journey back to Coimbatore.

  • Checkout and load luggage
  • Optional brief stop in Mysore (Palace external view / lunch)
  • Comfortable drop-off at Coimbatore (Airport/Railway/Home)
