Valparai Premium Tour

Experience the thrill of the famous 40 hairpin bends and discover an untouched Western Ghats paradise. Valparai offers pristine tea estates, dense wildlife-rich forests, and breathtaking viewpoints, making it the perfect offbeat getaway from Coimbatore.

Tour Itinerary

01

The 40 Hairpin Ascent & Waterfalls

Depart from Coimbatore towards Pollachi. The adventure begins as we climb the incredibly scenic Anamalai hills. Enjoy several viewpoint stops as we navigate the iconic 40 hairpin bends to reach Valparai.

  • Aliyar Dam Viewpoint (Base of the hills)
  • Monkey Falls (Refreshing natural cascade)
  • Loam's View Point (9th Hairpin bend panoramic view)
  • Attakatti Viewpoint & Check-in to Valparai resort
02

Dams, Tea Estates & Departure

Wake up to the misty tea gardens of Valparai. We will spend the day exploring deep into the plateau, visiting massive dams and incredible viewpoints before making our descent back to Coimbatore.

  • Sholayar Dam (The second deepest dam in Asia)
  • Nallamudi Viewpoint (Overlooking the Anamudi peak)
  • Karamalai Annai Velankanni Church
  • Scenic descent and drop-off at Coimbatore
