The Ultimate Ooty Weekend Itinerary from Coimbatore

Ooty, the "Queen of Hill Stations," is the most requested weekend getaway for our clients at Get Taxi Kovai. Just an 85 km drive from Coimbatore, the journey itself is spectacular, winding through the Nilgiri hills. Here is our recommended 2-day itinerary for a perfect weekend.

Day 1: Classic Ooty

Start your journey early from Coimbatore (we recommend a 6:00 AM pickup). After navigating the scenic Mettupalayam ghat roads, your first stop should be the Government Botanical Gardens. Spend your afternoon enjoying a peaceful boat ride on Ooty Lake, followed by a visit to the Thread Garden. Wrap up your evening with homemade chocolates from the local markets.

Day 2: Scenic Landscapes

Dedicate your second day to the outskirts. Drive up to Doddabetta Peak for a panoramic view of the Nilgiris. Next, head towards Pykara to see the waterfalls and the tranquil Pykara Lake. On your way back down to Coimbatore, have your driver stop at the Pine Tree Forests for some incredible photos.

Pro Travel Tip

The ghat roads have 36 hairpin bends. Booking a premium, spacious vehicle like an Innova Crysta ensures a comfortable, nausea-free ride for your family.
